## MRI and Image Reconstruction | PyTorch, Signal Processing | [Full Repo](https://github.com/suzannasem/STAT-4830-mri-reconstruction-project)
Developed optimization-based reconstruction algorithms in PyTorch to recover MRI scans from synthetically under-sampled k-space data. Implemented Zero-filled Fast Fourier Transforms (FFT) as a baseline, improving using Gaussian and Laplacian kernel-based reconstructions as well as generative diffusion models to interpolate missing frequency data. Handled high-dimensional, complex-valued tensors, ensuring mathematical consistency between spatial and frequency domains. Collaborative project with Sydney Bergstrom, Aaron Meslin, and Amy Zhou.

## Investigating Adverse Drug Reactions | R, keras, XGBoost | [Full Repo](https://github.com/suzannasem/fda_reactions/tree/main)
Engineered a predictive pipeline using the FDA Adverse Event Reporting System (FAERS) to identify high-risk medication outcomes. Built and tuned high-performance classification models using XGBoost (Gradient Boosting) and Neural Networks (Keras). Performed feature selection and regularization using LASSO and Logistic Regression to identify key risk stabilizers. Cleaned large-scale, high-dimensional categorical datasets, addressing class imbalance through loss-function weighting. Collaborative project with Olivia Yang and Kruthi Muralidhara Jety.

## Modeling Lesion Growth and Infertility in Endometriosis | MATLAB | [Full Repo](https://github.com/suzannasem/endo)
Developed a mathematical model using systems of non-linear differential equations to simulate lesion growth in endometriosis. Leveraged MATLAB’s numerical integration suite (including ode15s) to resolve high-frequency oscillations and stiff system behaviors, ensuring 99%+ convergence accuracy. Engineered custom plotting scripts to analyze multi-dimensional parameter spaces and identify critical biological transitions. This is the first model of lesion growth in endometriosis to include female endocrine dynamics, follicular growth, and estrogen-receptor binding dynamics. We found that dysfunction of the ER-beta promoter caused disease onset and that reduced oocyte quality in endo-associated infertility may be explained by local estradiol synthesis by lesion cells.
